Club History Timeline

  1. Early Years & Amateur Football (1899 - c. 1989)

    • Founded on July 1, 1899, as a gymnastics club (Turnverein Hoffenheim)
    • 1921: Separate football club established (Fußballverein Hoffenheim)
    • 1945: Merger of TV and FV to form TSG 1899 Hoffenheim
    • Predominantly played in lower tiers of German football
    • Notable achievements in B-Klasse Sinsheim Nord (1957, 1961, 1970)

  2. The Beginning of Dietmar Hopp's Involvement (1989 - 2000)

    • 1989: SAP co-founder Dietmar Hopp begins financial support
    • 1990s: Steady climb through amateur leagues
    • 1999: Opening of the Dietmar-Hopp-Stadion
    • 2000: Promotion to Oberliga Baden-Württemberg

  3. Rise to Professional Football (2000 - 2008)

    • 2000/01: Promotion to Regionalliga Süd
    • 2006: Appointment of Ralf Rangnick as manager
    • 2007: Promotion to 2. Bundesliga
    • 2008: Historic promotion to Bundesliga

  4. Bundesliga Era (2008 - Present)

    • 2008/09: Sensational debut season, "Herbstmeister"
    • 2009: Opening of the PreZero Arena (formerly Rhein-Neckar-Arena)
    • 2017: First UEFA Champions League qualification
    • 2018: Best Bundesliga finish (3rd place)
    • 2023: Continued establishment as a stable Bundesliga club
